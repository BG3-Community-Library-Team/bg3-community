  • Filename: The name of the model in the actual game files.

  • UUID: The unique identifier for the model.
  • NPC: Which NPC uses the specific model.

Human/Half-Elf/Elf/Drow

Player Meshes

The meshes above are used for Humans/Half-Elves/Elves/Drows bodies and therefore any change will apply to all these races.

However, Humans/Half-Elves and Elves/Drows have different textures assigned to them, most noticably the .CLEA map where Elves/Drows lack hair.

NPC Meshes

Unique Elf/Drow UUIDS

Some characters have Unique mesh UUID even if they use the same mesh as the default, as they may have different Material/Skeleton overrides than the player's.

Tiefling

Player Meshes

Tail Meshes

The meshes for tieflings are slightly different from Humans/Half-Elves/Elves/Drows meshes: 

  1. A different neck seam, Human/Half-Elf/Elf/Drow heads will not apply naturally and vice versa.
  2. A hole at the lower back where the tail should be.
  3. Prodding underdeveloped wing shoulderblades.
  4. Claws instead of nails.
