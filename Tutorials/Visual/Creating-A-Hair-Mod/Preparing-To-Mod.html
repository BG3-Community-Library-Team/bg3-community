So, you wanna make your own hair mod for Baldur's Gate 3? You wanna create your own mesh and then get it coded into the game as a standalone CC option?

Well, hold on a second--before any of that, you're gonna need some tools.

Important Tools

  1. Blender (version 3.6 recommended)
    • For importing, editing, and exporting the hair mesh itself
  2. Lslib by Norbyte
    • For conforming your custom hair to a vanilla hair
  3. BG3 Multi-Tool by ShinyHobo
    • For searching the game files
  4. Collada/GR2 import/export Blender plugin by Norbyte
    • For importing/exporting .GR2 files, which BG3 uses
  5. VSCode or Notepad++
    • For editing code files

Choosing meshes to work with

The easiest and most straightforward way of making custom BG3 hair is to take pieces of vanilla hairs and combine them into something new. So, you're gonna need to choose which vanilla hairs you'd like to use.
