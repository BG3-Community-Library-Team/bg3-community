This is a guide that covers the ENTIRE workflow of making an armour mod.

It’s about 10 pages long, and pretty detailed, so it might take at least 15 minutes to read.  

The reason why I’m writing this guide is that, I want to provide a step-by-step general instruction for armor modding, so people won’t have to find tutorial A here tutorial B there tutorial C somewhere else…without any guidance, just like what I had to go through in the beginning.

So umm…I hope this helps! And I hope that this guide will become one of those articles that people might save bookmarks of, and then come back to it when they forgot one step in their workflow, or simply couldn’t figure out what’s wrong and just wanna overview the whole process…that’s it! :D

(I will put the required tools and other resources at the bottom of this article, so that you can decide what to use and what not to)
