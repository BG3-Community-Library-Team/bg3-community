Patch 7 Prevention 

This guide is for players with mods who need simple, step-by-step instructions on how to prevent downloading the patch and being locked out of their saves due to mods that are not updated yet, so they can continue to play their pre-patch game with their existing mods.
 

If you already launch directly from the .exe without opening a mo  manager, you can skip the sections for BG3MM and Vortex.


Stopping Steam or GOG from automatically starting on boot

Stopping the program from starting when you boot your computer will help prevent immediate accidental patching.

  1. Open Task Manager on your computer (ctrl+shift+esc at the same time on your keyboard)
  2. Go to the Startup tab.
  3. Find Steam or GOG Galaxy in the list. Right-click it and click Disable.
  4. On your next reboot, Steam or GOG will no longer automatically start with your computer, preventing accidental patching.

 

Disable game auto-updates

Stopping the program from automatically updating the game helps prevent accidental patching if you happen to open Steam or GOG for any reason.

Steam users

  1. Open Steam
  2. Go to Library
  3. Right-Click Baldur's Gate 3 from your games on the left and click Properties
  4. Under Updates, beneath Automatic Updates, click the drop-down and choose Only update this game when I launch it 

GOG Galaxy users

  1. Open GOG Galaxy.
  2. Click the cogwheel icon at the top left and click Settings.
  3. Go to the Game Features option on the left.
  4. Click the box to uncheck Auto-update games.

BG3MM users

Open BG3MM.

  1. Open Settings.
  2. Find Skip Launcher and enable by clicking the box.
  3. Find Launch through Steam (below Skip Launcher) and disable by clicking the box.
  4. Click the Save icon at the top left of the Preferences window.
  5. To play the game, click the small BG3 icon, or use the EXE directly in your bin folder.

When ready to patch, launch the game from Steam again.

 

Vortex Users

Vortex launches the game directly with the EXE without Steam or GOG, so no configuration is required.

Do not click Check for Updates until you are ready to fully update to patch 7 for both the game and your mods.
