Patch 7 Prevention (or How to Launch BG3 without Steam for Dummies) 

This guide is for players with mods using Steam and BG3 Mod Manager (BG3MM). This guide does not work for Vortex users.
 

  1. Stop Steam from starting with your computer.
    1. Open Task Manager on your computer (ctrl+shift+esc at the same time on your keyboard)
    2. Go to the Startup tab.
    3. Find Steam in the list. Right-click it and select Disable.
    4. On your next reboot, Steam will no longer automatically start with your computer, preventing accidental patching.
  2. Open BG3MM.
  3. Open Settings.
  4. Find Skip Launcher and enable by clicking the box.
  5. Find Launch through Steam (below Skip Launcher) and disable by clicking the box.
  6. Click the Save icon at the top left of the Preferences window.
  7. To play the game, click the small BG3 icon, or use the EXE directly in your bin folder.

When ready to patch, launch the game from Steam again.
